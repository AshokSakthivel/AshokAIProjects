Conway's Law in Action — Your Team IS Your Architecture
🔬 The Inverse Conway Maneuver
We don't design a new architecture and hope the org follows. We first reshape the org to match the architecture we want — then the system design emerges naturally from team boundaries. Each team owns one bounded context. Their API contract is the service interface.
